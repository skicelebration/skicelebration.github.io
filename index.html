About Ski Celebration

“Build your reputation by helping others build theirs.” –Anthony J. D’Angelo

As the fastest growing Ski Planner and High Adventure Outfitter in the Southeast, we pride ourselves in serving youth groups!

We understand the importance of developing and nurturing every relationship we have with our prospective and existing clients.

Why we are different?

First- By providing the best equipment on the mountain at the lowest price, we are committed to helping you stretch your youth ministry budget.

Second- Our complimentary “Pre-Fit” program continues to be a huge hit with youth ministries. Prior to your retreat, we will come to your church and pre-fit your group. That way, when you arrive at our ski shop, we’ll have your boots, skis & snowboards ready-to-go (with your names on them)… Allowing your youth to hit the slopes quicker than ever!

Third- If your youth enjoy skiing and rafting, you’ll love our “Ski Now/Raft Later” Loyalty Rewards Program! For every two Winterplace lifts and Ski Celebration rentals, you’ll earn one rafting voucher that can be used next year on the Ocoee, Nantahala or Chattahoochee Rivers (i.e. 50 skiers equals 25 rafting vouchers).

Fourth- We are a service-oriented company charged with the excitement of helping young people discover the outdoors, cultivate their friendships, and strengthen their relationship with Christ.
